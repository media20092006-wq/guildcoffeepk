Our Story

Our Process, Your Ritual

Guild Coffee was born from a simple belief: great coffee brings people together. We started in the heart of PECHS, Karachi — a small space where founders, creators, and dreamers could gather over a perfectly pulled shot.

Today, we remain committed to ethical sourcing, small-batch roasting, and precision brewing. Every cup is a collaboration between farmer, roaster, and barista — crafted to bring you not just caffeine, but clarity.
